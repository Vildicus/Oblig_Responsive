INNOVATION CAMP

Innovation camp is for everyone who is interested in innovation. Participants will be given a challenge where they will generate and evaluate ideas and solutions and create a business model for the specific problem they want to solve. Through the camp you will be thought fun and effective ways to think more creative and how to build good projects from scratch.
