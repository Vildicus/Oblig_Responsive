Inova was recently established to spread our mentors experience with frugal innovation. Frugal innovation is when the complexity and cost of a production is reduced, and that way deliver grate value for money while at the same time the impact on scarce resources is reduced. Our mentors have long previous experience with frugal innovation and want to spread this successful business model of affordability, simplicity, quality, and sustainability by hosting innovation camps.
